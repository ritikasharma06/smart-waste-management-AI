Smart Dustbin with IoT Sensors

Intelligent waste containers that prevent overflow and optimize collection routes

How It Works

  • Ultrasonic sensor measures the garbage height and calculates fill percentage.
  • Mobile alert is triggered when the bin reaches high capacity to notify collection teams.
  • Motion sensor optionally opens the lid automatically for users.
  • Overflow prevention keeps public spaces clean by alerting before the bin overflows.
